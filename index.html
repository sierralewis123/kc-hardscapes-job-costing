Monthly Overhead Data

Enter your total business expenses and total direct labor hours worked by your crew for each month. For each job, overhead is calculated per month as: (this job's hours that month ÷ total company hours that month) × rolling 3-month avg expenses The 3-month rolling average uses the three months immediately before the job's month (whatever exist), so you're allocating overhead based on recent settled expenses instead of the current in-progress month. If a job spans two months, the two allocations are summed together.
